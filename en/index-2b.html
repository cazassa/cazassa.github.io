Consultancy in Deals

>>>What is the right price of my land?<<<

Any land presents different value ​​depending on the use.

Use for retail is generally more favorable to the owner of land. However, sales for residential and commercial projects is most appropriate depending on circumstances such as the coefficient of construction (multiples of construction) and inclination of the region.

For a typical residential development, it is necessary to calculate the number of square meters or square feet to be built. Based on this potential and the construction costs and other costs such as commercial and profit margins of those involved, it is possible to determine the appropriate price of land for residential development.

Contact us for a tailored solution.

>>>How profitable is a logistics warehouse?<<<

Data from consultancy firms Cushman & Wakefield and Colliers indicate that the lease price of a Logistics Warehouse in Brazil is between R$15 and R$30 per square meter per month.

Depending on the quality of construction, project location, size, access, etc., the warehouses are valued more if you are in the metropolitan areas (distant up to 150km from the main centers) and with access to major roads and junctions.

The value of the square meter in the state of SP can reach R$30/sq.m./month and ensures returns as high as 15%pa for the proprietor or investor. In case of sale of these assets, there is a multiplier that can reach 10x your annual income value, indicating a capitalization rate (cap rate) of 10%.
