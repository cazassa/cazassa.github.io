Below we list the main demands for fundraising.

Checklist:
1. Legal structure and organogram of the Company;

2. Consolidated Financial Reports of the last three years, with explanatory notes and audit report (if audited);

3. Track record of execution (at least 3 years)

4. Feasibility study with cash flow (revenues, costs and expenses) and schedule (land acquisition, expected approval date, launch date, construction works start and conclusion);

5. Market analysis (with competitors);

6. Relevant local information (income level, demographics, etc.);

7. Main points of interest in the neighboring region (means of transportation, access, businesses, schools, hospitals, etc.);

8. List of units to be constructed (with usable / sellable area of each unit);

9. Blueprints for the project and units (including desired design);

10. Economic and finance viability conditions (shareholders, partners and banking relationship).

 

We can support companies in organizing above mentioned information.

>>>How to structure a MBS (CRI) in Brazil?<<<

CRI (MBS) is a simple form of structured credit in which financial gains are directed to an investor who anticipates the resources to the interested company. Grants can apply such as escrow account related to undergoing construction works, among others.

To structure a CRI it is necessary to have a portfolio of mortgage-backed securities that have a flow of future payments, desirably above 2 years of average time of future cash flow.

Follows a small checklist of a Securitization:

- Payment history with at least 6 months

- Debit balance / value of units (ratio LTV) below 85%

- Controlled Delinquency

- Approval process for sale and collection rule

- Permission to securitize the purchase and sale agreement of the client or contract of the principal debtor
